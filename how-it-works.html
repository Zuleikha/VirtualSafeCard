How Our Virtual Card System Works

A simple, secure way to protect your primary accounts while shopping online

The Virtual Card Process

1

Sign Up & Verification

Creating your account is quick and straightforward:

  • Register with your email address and create a secure password
  • Complete a simple identity verification process to comply with security regulations
  • Set up two-factor authentication for added account security

Our verification process is designed to be as frictionless as possible while ensuring compliance with global KYC (Know Your Customer) requirements.

2

Fund Your Wallet

Add funds to your secure digital wallet using your preferred method:

  • Bank transfer
  • PayPal
  • Existing debit or credit card
  • Other digital payment methods (varies by region)

Your funds are securely held and can be used to create multiple virtual cards. You can add funds as needed or set up automatic top-ups.

3

Create Virtual Cards

Generate virtual cards instantly based on your needs:

  • Single-Use Cards: Perfect for one-time purchases, these cards automatically close after the first transaction
  • Multi-Use Cards: For repeated purchases or subscriptions, these cards remain active until you close them
  • Merchant-Locked Cards: Cards that can only be used with specific merchants for enhanced security

Each virtual card comes with its own unique card number, expiration date, and CVV code, just like a physical card.

4

Set Controls & Limits

Customize your virtual cards with powerful control features:

  • Set maximum spending limits per transaction
  • Define daily, weekly, or monthly spending caps
  • Enable or disable specific merchant categories
  • Set geographic restrictions if needed

These controls give you unprecedented flexibility and security compared to traditional payment methods.

5

Shop Online Securely

Use your virtual cards for online purchases just like any regular card:

  • Enter the virtual card details at checkout
  • Use our browser extension to auto-fill card details securely
