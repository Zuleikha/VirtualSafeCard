Card Types & Generation

Instant Card Generation

Create virtual cards on demand with unique card numbers, expiration dates, and CVV codes. Our system generates cards instantly, allowing you to shop online without delay.

Single-Use Cards

Perfect for one-time purchases on unfamiliar websites. These cards automatically close after the first transaction, eliminating the risk of unauthorized recurring charges or data breaches.

Multi-Use Cards

Standard virtual cards that can be used for multiple purchases. Ideal for trusted merchants or recurring payments, these cards remain active until you choose to close them.

Merchant-Locked Cards

Restrict cards to specific merchants for enhanced security. Even if card details are compromised, they cannot be used elsewhere, providing an additional layer of protection.

Recurring Payment Support

Dedicated cards for subscription services with special handling to ensure uninterrupted service while maintaining security and control over your recurring payments.

Bulk Card Creation

Generate multiple virtual cards simultaneously for different purposes, saving time when preparing for various online purchases or subscription services.

Spending Controls

Transaction Limits

Set maximum spending limits per transaction to prevent large unauthorized charges. Perfect for controlling exactly how much can be spent in a single purchase.

Time-Based Limits

Define daily, weekly, or monthly spending caps to maintain budget control and add an extra layer of security to your virtual cards.

Card Lifecycle Management

Instantly pause, resume, or permanently close virtual cards with a single click, giving you complete control over when and how your cards can be used.

Merchant Category Restrictions

Limit card usage to specific merchant categories, preventing unauthorized use in unintended business types or potentially fraudulent sectors.

Geographic Restrictions

Add location-based controls to your virtual cards, limiting usage to specific countries or regions for additional security against international fraud.

Expiration Date Control

Set custom expiration dates for your virtual cards, ensuring they automatically deactivate after a predetermined period even without manual intervention.
