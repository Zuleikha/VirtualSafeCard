Regulatory Compliance

Our commitment to meeting global financial regulations while protecting your data and privacy

Compliance Framework

Operating a global virtual card system requires adherence to a complex web of financial regulations and security standards. Our comprehensive compliance framework is designed to meet or exceed requirements across multiple jurisdictions while providing a seamless user experience.

Our regulatory approach is built on four key pillars:

  • Know Your Customer (KYC) & Anti-Money Laundering (AML): Robust identity verification and transaction monitoring to prevent financial crimes while protecting legitimate users.
  • Payment Processing Licenses & PCI DSS: Proper licensing and adherence to the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard to ensure secure handling of payment card information.
  • Data Privacy & Security: Compliance with global data protection regulations like GDPR and CCPA to safeguard user information.
  • Multi-Jurisdictional Approach: Strategies for navigating the complex regulatory landscape across different countries and regions.

This framework allows us to operate globally while maintaining the highest standards of security, privacy, and regulatory compliance. We continuously monitor regulatory changes and update our systems and processes accordingly to ensure ongoing compliance.

Detailed Compliance Information

Know Your Customer (KYC) & Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

Virtual card systems face unique KYC and AML challenges due to their digital nature and potential for misuse. Our approach combines advanced technology with robust processes to ensure compliance while maintaining a smooth user experience.

Key Challenges We Address:

  • Identity Fraud Risk: The digital-only nature of our service, without face-to-face interaction, increases the risk of identity fraud. We implement advanced verification methods to mitigate this risk.
  • Deepfake Technology: AI-powered deepfakes pose a significant threat to remote identity verification. Our systems include specialized detection methods to combat this emerging threat.
  • Transactional Fraud: The speed and ease of virtual card issuance could potentially be exploited for money laundering through numerous small transactions. Our monitoring systems are designed to detect such patterns.
  • Cross-Border Complexity: Operating globally means navigating varying KYC/AML standards across jurisdictions, requiring a sophisticated compliance approach.

Our KYC/AML Solutions:

  • Advanced Identity Verification: We employ multi-layered identity verification, including document validation, biometric matching, and liveness detection to ensure users are who they claim to be.
  • AI-Powered Screening: Automated screening against sanctions lists, Politically Exposed Persons (PEP) lists, and adverse media using advanced AI algorithms.
  • Real-time Transaction Monitoring: Sophisticated AI systems analyze transaction patterns to detect suspicious activity, with significantly improved fraud detection rates and reduced false positives.
