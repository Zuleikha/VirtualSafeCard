Comprehensive Security Features

1

Primary Account Protection

The core security benefit of our virtual card system is that your primary financial accounts remain completely isolated from online merchants and potential data breaches:

  • Separation Layer: Virtual cards create a secure barrier between your primary accounts and online merchants, ensuring your actual bank account or credit card details are never exposed during transactions.
  • Limited Exposure: Even if a virtual card is compromised, only the funds allocated to that specific card are at risk, not your entire bank account or credit line.
  • Disposable Cards: Single-use virtual cards automatically close after one transaction, eliminating the risk of recurring unauthorized charges.
  • Merchant-Specific Cards: Cards can be locked to specific merchants, rendering them useless if stolen and attempted to be used elsewhere.

This isolation strategy is the foundation of our security architecture, dramatically reducing the potential impact of data breaches and fraud.
